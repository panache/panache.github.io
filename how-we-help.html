How We Help

We built the fund we
wished existed when
we were founders.

Two GPs who've built companies, screened thousands of deals, and sat on both sides of the table. Here's what that means for you.

01 // Capital
The first cheque. Without the circus.
Core cheques of $150k–$1M. Scout cheques of $25k for the very earliest signal. We lead rounds and take 5–7.5% ownership. Our investment committee runs on a fixed weekly cadence — you'll have a decision within two weeks of your first meeting. Every time.
02 // Network
A world-class alumni network, activated.
1 in 4 Canadian unicorn founders graduated from Waterloo. We're embedded at the centre of that ecosystem — inside Velocity Incubator, backed by the UW Endowment. Our LP base includes founders and operators from Instacart to Flutterwave to ApplyBoard. Your next customer or co-investor is probably already in our network.
03 // Fundraising
We make your next raise easier.
We've sat on both sides of the table. When it's time for your next round, we help you sharpen your narrative, prepare materials, and make warm introductions to the right funds. Our portfolio companies don't fundraise in the dark.
04 // Honesty
Blunt feedback. The real answer.
We tell you what we actually think — about your pricing, positioning, team, and narrative. Founders who work with us describe us as the investor they call when they want the real answer. We operate like a co-founder, not a board member.
The Process

A decision in
2 weeks.
Guaranteed.

Our investment committee meets on a fixed weekly cadence. We commit on the second Thursday after our first meeting. No ambiguity, no "we'll circle back." You always know exactly where you stand — yes or a clear no.

We share our diligence questions upfront. No surprises, no last-minute asks. If we're going to pass, we tell you why and we tell you fast.
